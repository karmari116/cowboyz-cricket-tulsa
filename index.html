About Us

Cowboyz Cricket Club is a Tulsa, Oklahoma registered sports club with a mission to promote the game of cricket in Tulsa by involving both novice and experienced members in practice and match situations.

We participate in the Two-State Cricket League every year, competing in both T35 overs and T20 formats. Our matches take us across different cities including Oklahoma City, Wichita (Kansas), and Tulsa (Oklahoma). Our home ground is located at Terwilliger Park, 5902 W 10th St, Tulsa, OK 74127.

We welcome everyone from the Tulsa community! Whether you're a local resident, a student, or new to the area, our club is open to all cricket enthusiasts. We especially encourage Tulsa locals and students from universities across the Tulsa area to join us. Students from Oklahoma State University and other local institutions participate every year, bringing fresh energy and talent to our team.

Premier League Experience

Special mention to our players from Bentonville, Arkansas, who participate in our league.
